THOMAS P. COX, AIA

cox@tca-arch.com

Thomas P. Cox, AIA is the Managing Partner and CEO at TCA with over 25 years of design and leadership experience. Mr. Cox has successfully positioned TCA as a recognized industry leader in architecture, planning and urban design. The firm specializes in high-density urban infill and mixed-use projects throughout the western United States.

In his role as CEO, Mr. Cox is responsible for the overall design direction of the firm as well as business development and corporate management. Under his guidance, TCA is currently designing a large number of significant projects in a variety of T densities and locations for many of the nation's largest and most recognized developers.

Mr. Cox and the TCA design team have garnered a number of prestigious industry awards from AIA, PCBC, Urban Land Institute, NAHB-MultiFamily, Historical Society, SAM and MAME. Mr. Cox is also a regular on the speaking circuit giving speeches on high-rise design and construction, evolving floorplans and urban planning.
