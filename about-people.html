Firm Profile

Established in 1993, TCA is a corporate award-winning architectural firm recognized as a leader in the planning and design of housing environments hroughout the western United States and overseas.

Our 2 studios located in Los Angeles and Irvine, California; house our 5 Principals overseeing a highly qualified design team. Specializing in high density multifamily housing, our portfolio also includes: Student and Senior Housing, Affordable, Mixed-use and Transit Oriented projects. TCA also has a strong background in Master Planning and Community Design.

In his role as CEO, Mr. Cox is responsible for the overall design direction of the firm as well as business development and corporate management. Under his guidance, TCA is currently designing a large number of significant projects in a variety of T densities and locations for many of the nation's largest and most recognized developers.

Our international experience includes a wide-range of affordable and market-rate, mid and high-rise apartments, condominiums, townhomes; as well as

mixed-use and resort experience in Korea, Saudi Arabia, and the Philippines.

TCA has long been a proponent of Sustainable Building practices. With an in-house staff of 13 LEED-accredited professionals, TCA strives to incorporate intelligent and cost-effective “green” technologies into all of our designs.

We believe that building design is a collaborative process. We enjoy working in teams, and value the relationships cultivated in them. It is our goal to have everyone associated with the project to be pleased with the results and share equally in the success. We are proud of our extensive list of returning clients that demonstrate our exceptional service.
